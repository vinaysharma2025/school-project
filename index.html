Delhi Public School, Jaipur, Ajmer Road Branch NH-8, Jaipur Ajmer

Delhi Public School, Jaipur runs under the aegis of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan. It is one of the premier institutions of Rajasthan which was established in 1985. It is spread over an area of 17.5 acres of land. The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education with classes from pre-primary to XII. Since its inception, Bhavan’s Vidyashram has strived to provide quality education that focuses on academic excellence, active learning and commitment to the environment. It also strives to draw inspiration from the rich and diverse cultural heritage of India and gives the students, independence in thoughts, life skills and a competitive edge required to meet the challenges.

The institution runs on the following principles which are at the core of Bhavan’s Philosophy:

* Preservation of the Cultural Heritage.

* Strengthening of National Unity and Integrity.

* Character Building and Inculcation of Moral as well as Ethical Values.

* Protection of Environment and Conservation of Natural Resources.

The school offers the best infrastructure for sports in its sprawling campus. The school has one of the best Sports Complexes in Rajasthan and the International Standard Swimming Pool churns out a number of medalists every year. Special coaching by experts is provided to the students. The campus also has state-of-the-art facilities for Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, Hockey, Volleyball and Cricket. The school teams excel in various District, State and National Level tournaments. Students are also trained in Athletics, Kho-Kho, Skating, Handball and Gymnastics.
